10m and 15m Essay Writing

Workflow for Students

  1. Paste the essay question.
  2. Write your own outline first.
  3. Click AI Check Outline.
  4. Use the feedback to write Requirement 1 paragraph.
  5. Click AI Check Requirement 1.
  6. Use the feedback to write Requirement 2 paragraph.
  7. Click AI Check Requirement 2.
  8. Click Generate Full Analysis.
  9. For 15m part b questions, write your evaluation.
  10. Click AI Check Evaluation.
